What is this Book?

How It Went Down by Kekla Magoon is a thought-provoking novel that investigates the aftermath of a police shooting that occurs in a residential neighborhood. It is told from the perspective of multiple characters who recount their own versions of events, creating a tapestry of perspectives on the shooting, the victim, Tariq Johnson, and his community. The book provides a raw and emotive glimpse into the complex issues of race, social inequality, and police brutality. Magoon's vivid writing style, complex characters, and multifaceted plot make this an engaging and thought-provoking read that is sure to resonate with any reader.
